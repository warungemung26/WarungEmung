Privacy Policy

Warung Emung

Last updated: January 2026

Warung Emung (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a web-based application (Progressive Web App) designed to help users order daily groceries conveniently through WhatsApp.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting users’ personal information.

Information We Collect

Warung Emung does not collect, store, or process any personal user data directly.

  • No user account or login is required
  • No names, addresses, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected
  • No user database is maintained

WhatsApp Usage

Warung Emung provides buttons or links that redirect users to WhatsApp to place orders.

All interactions on WhatsApp are managed entirely by WhatsApp Inc. We do not have access to the content of WhatsApp conversations. WhatsApp’s own Privacy Policy applies.

Technical Information

We do not intentionally collect IP addresses, GPS location data, or device-specific information.

Like most web services, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may automatically log standard technical information for security and performance purposes.

Cookies

Warung Emung does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the service using HTTPS and secure hosting infrastructure. However, no online service is completely secure.

Children’s Information

Warung Emung is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
